Raising Precious Children who are Total & Complete

Total Child School is a Christian co-educational institution with Nursery, Primary, and Secondary sections, committed to raising children who are total and complete. We began as a preschool in 2016 and expanded into full school operations in 2017.

Our aim is simple: **To groom children who will in the near future be in positions of influence in Nigeria and beyond.** We show our pupils possibilities and walk with them through every stage of growth.

Our Core Values

★
