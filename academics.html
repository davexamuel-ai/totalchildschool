Our Academic Programs

A holistic education structured for lifelong success

🧸

Nursery Section

Our Nursery section focuses on nurturing young minds through play-based and child-centred teaching practices. We utilize Montessori methods, Jolly Phonics, and sensory exploration to spark curiosity, creativity, and a genuine love for learning from the very start.

Classes: Pearl, Ruby, Emerald

📚

Primary Section

At the Primary level, we balance strict academic excellence with cultural values and student development. Students cover core subjects alongside cultural courses, including Yoruba Language, creating engaging and inclusive environments where they grow both academically and morally.

Classes: Sapphire to Turquoise

🎓

Secondary Section

Our Secondary department delivers a seasoned curriculum that grooms critical thinking, discipline, and national competitiveness. We nurture pupils in leadership, scientific inquiry, and technology, preparing them to enter positions of influence locally and globally.

Classes: Amber to Diamond
