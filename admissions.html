The Admission Process

We welcome applications from children of all backgrounds who seek academic rigor and character development. Here is how it works:

1

Submit Inquiry

Fill out the online admission inquiry form below or visit our campus. We will schedule a personalized tour for you and your child.

2

Assessment & Interview

Prospective students undergo an age-appropriate entrance assessment (written and oral) to place them at their exact level of understanding.

3

Enrollment

Upon successful assessment, you will receive an official admission offer. Complete registration documents and pay enrollment fees to secure your spot.

Admission Inquiries

Have questions about fees, curriculum, or enrollment slots? Please fill out our inquiry form. Our admissions team is dedicated to guiding you through every step of your child’s enrollment.
