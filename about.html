About Our School

Nurturing complete individuals since 2016

Our History & Christian Ethos

Total Child School began its journey in **2016** as a dedicated preschool. Guided by our strong vision to provide a total and complete upbringing, we expanded into full school operations in **2017**. Built firmly on a Christian ethos, we focus on helping every child grow in wisdom, stature, and favor.

We believe that every child is a unique gem, full of promise. Our role is to create the environment where they can discover, develop, and deploy their potential. From creche to graduation, we offer structured mentorship and academic excellence to ensure they stand out in every sphere of life.

Our Vision

"To develop the child to stand out in every sphere of life, to be total and complete."

Our Aim

"To groom children who will in the near future be in positions of influence in Nigeria and beyond. We show our pupils possibilities."

Mr. Toyin Ibitoye is a respected leader, strategic communicator, and passionate advocate for children and youth development. He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Agricultural Extension Science from the University of Ibadan and a Postgraduate Diploma in Sports Management from the prestigious FIFA/CIES International Programme, in partnership with Nelson Mandela Metropolitan University, Port Elizabeth, South South Africa.

His professional journey spans media, public service, and national representation. He served as Media Officer to the Super Eagles and was Press Officer for Team Nigeria at the 2022 Commonwealth Games in Birmingham, United Kingdom. Between 2021 and 2023, he served as Special Assistant on Media to the Honourable Minister of Youth and Sports Development, Mr. Sunday Dare, under the administration of former President Muhammadu Buhari.

A multiple award-winning broadcaster, Toyin was recognized in 2013 as Nigeria’s Best Television Football Journalist by the Nigeria Football Pitch Awards — an honor celebrated on FIFA’s official website. Through his work, he has consistently championed discipline, teamwork, excellence, and mentorship.

As Chairman of Total Child School, Toyin provides visionary governance and strategic oversight. He is committed to ensuring the school remains a nurturing environment where Total Children develop strong character, critical thinking, and the confidence to thrive locally and globally. His leadership philosophy is simple: “Every child deserves the opportunity to discover, develop, and deploy their God-given potential.”

Mrs. Adetola Ibitoye is the heart of teaching and learning at Total Child School. With over 27 years of experience spanning media, education, and child development, she brings a rare blend of academic depth, practical classroom expertise, and a genuine love for children.

She holds a B.Sc in Mass Communication from the University of Lagos and a Postgraduate Diploma in Education from Corona College of Education. As a Goldman Sachs Scholar, she earned a Certificate in Entrepreneurship from Pan-Atlantic University. She is also certified by the British Education Council through the UK Teacher Certification Programme.

A trained Montessori educator and licensed teacher with the Teachers Registration Council of Nigeria (TRCN), Mrs. Ibitoye is passionate about early childhood development and learner-centered education. She is currently pursuing her Cambridge Curriculum Mastery Programme to further strengthen our international standards.

Through continuous professional development, Mrs. Ibitoye ensures that Total Child School remains a place where curiosity is sparked, character is built, and every child is taught at their level of understanding. Her leadership philosophy is simple: “When a child is taught well, they can learn anything.”

Mr. Adeleke Adekunle Raymond is an experienced School leader with over 16 years’ experience across teaching, administration, and school leadership. He brings a wealth of expertise to strengthen our commitment to academic excellence and child-centered care.

He is a passionate educator with a strong background in English Language, Mass Communication, and School Administration. His professional training has taken him across Nigeria, Spain, the USA, Benin Republic, the UK, and China — giving him broad, global insight into best practices in education.

Mr. Adeleke has served successfully as HOD, Vice-Principal, Principal, Head of School, School Counselor, and Educational Manager. His focus is simple: create a safe, inclusive, and high-achieving environment where every child thrives, teachers grow, and parents feel confident.

Mr. Davids Remilekun is a seasoned education professional and the Head of the Secondary Department. He holds a Bachelor's Degree in Educational Management, a Nigeria Certificate in Education (NCE), and a National Diploma (ND). With over 15 years of teaching experience, he has built a distinguished career marked by dedication, professionalism, and a passion for academic excellence.

Throughout his career, Mr. Remilekun has demonstrated strong leadership in curriculum implementation, staff supervision, student mentoring, and the promotion of high academic standards. He is committed to creating a supportive learning environment that nurtures discipline, critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning.

As the Head of the Secondary Department, he works closely with teachers, students, and parents to ensure quality education and holistic student development. He believes that every learner has the potential to succeed when guided with integrity, encouragement, and effective teaching.

Mrs. Akindele Oluwaseyi is a highly experienced and dedicated educator with a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Accounting and currently pursuing a Postgraduate Diploma in Education at the National Open University of Nigeria (NOUN). She possesses extensive teaching experience across all educational levels, from Nursery to Secondary School, with a strong ability to adapt instructional methods to suit diverse learners.

She is skilled in teaching a wide range of subjects, including Yoruba Language, and is committed to promoting academic excellence, cultural values, and student development. She demonstrates strong classroom management, communication, and organizational skills, with a proven ability to create engaging and inclusive learning environments.

She currently serves as the Primary School Sectional Head at Total Child School, providing effective leadership, mentoring teachers, and supporting curriculum development to enhance overall school performance.

Mrs. Beauty Ogheneruona Diavban is a dedicated and experienced early childhood educator with over 10 years of teaching and educational leadership experience. She holds a Postgraduate Diploma in Education (PGDE) and is passionate about nurturing young minds through innovative, child-centred teaching practices.

As the Nursery Sectional Head at Total Child School, Mrs. Diavban provides strategic leadership for the nursery section, ensuring the delivery of high-quality early years education in a safe, caring, and stimulating environment. She is committed to fostering children's academic, social, emotional, and moral development, helping every learner build a strong foundation for lifelong success.

Her professional development includes certifications in Early Childhood Education, Montessori Classroom Activities, Jolly Phonics, and other specialized areas of early years learning. These qualifications enable her to implement engaging, play-based, and evidence-based teaching strategies that inspire curiosity, creativity, confidence, and a genuine love for learning.

Mrs. Diavban is a registered member of the Teachers Registration Council of Nigeria (TRCN) and remains committed to continuous professional growth and educational excellence.
